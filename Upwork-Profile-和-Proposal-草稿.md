## Overview

I help small teams and solo operators turn repetitive digital work into faster, more reliable delivery.

My strongest project types are:

1. custom automation scripts for file, csv, and repetitive text workflows
2. turning messy notes, documents, and links into structured reports
3. setting up lightweight AI-assisted internal workflows

I focus on practical outcomes:

1. clear scope
2. fast first version
3. reusable outputs

I am a strong fit when you already know the pain point, but need someone to turn it into a working workflow or deliverable.
